OUR MENU

Espresso

Espresso is a concentrated coffee brewed by forcing a small amount of nearly boiling water through finely-ground coffee beans. It has a strong flavor and a velvety texture, making it the base for many other coffee drinks

Cappuccino

Cappuccino is a popular Italian coffee drink made with equal parts of espresso, steamed milk, and milk foam. It has a creamy texture and is often topped with a sprinkle of cocoa powder or cinnamon.

Latte

Latte is a coffee drink made with espresso and steamed milk. It has a smooth and creamy texture, with a milder coffee flavor compared to cappuccino. Lattes can be flavored with syrups such as vanilla, caramel, or hazelnut.

Americano

Americano is a diluted espresso drink made by adding hot water to espresso. It has a similar strength to drip coffee but retains the rich flavor and crema of espresso
